Andong Li 李安冬

I am currently a postgraduate student at Institute of Acoustics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IACAS) supervised by Prof.Chengshi Zheng and Prof.Xiaodong Li. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from the school of information science and engineering at Southeast University in 2018.

Research Interest

I am currently interested in the front-end speech techniques, including speech enhancement, dereverberation, and acoustic echo cancellation.
